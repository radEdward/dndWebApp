Welcome to an easy-to-use virtual table top for your role playing game needs. Click anywhere to make this window disappear.

I made this as a virtual game board to be displayed on a large monitor/television for your players while you run the game. Choose one of the test maps from the drop down menu above. Once you've chosen a map, click Load Map. An info button is available to walk you through the controls.

I'm moving this site to a new webspace soon, and you'll have the ability to upload any map image to use as your game board.

The icon images are a part of Devin Night's free token pack. The test maps are from Dyson's Dodecahedron
